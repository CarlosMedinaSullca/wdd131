Tarija Bolivia

Tarija is a small city in the south of Bolivia. It is recognized in the country because the production of grapes, chamomile and oregano. In the last 20 years there were big gas sources in the city, for many years it was one of the main economonic strength of the country. Currently the resources of gas have decreased and new explorations are being performed.
